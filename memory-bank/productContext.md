# Product Context

## Problem

Locksmiths need a secure, structured way to manage master key systems, cylinders, bittings, pinning, keyholders, rekeys, and reports without relying on paper records, spreadsheets, or expensive legacy software.

## Initial User

The initial user is a single locksmith/operator managing local master key systems for personal or small-business use.

## Future Users

Future users may include small locksmith shops, institutional locksmiths, facility managers, and local access-control operators.

## User Experience Goals

- Fast to navigate.
- Secure by default.
- Professional and calm.
- Useful on desktop first.
- Responsive enough for tablet and field use.
- Sensitive values hidden until intentionally revealed.
- Clear reports and worksheets.
- No accidental plaintext exposure.
- No unnecessary SaaS dependency in Version 1.

## Product Personality

KeyForge should feel like a secure locksmith operations dashboard. It should be calm, technical, organized, and trustworthy. It should not feel like a consumer toy, flashy marketing site, generic admin template, or pure spreadsheet clone.
